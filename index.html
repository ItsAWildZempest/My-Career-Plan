Computer & Information Systems Managers

This is the first career choice I picked. Mainly due to the money being nice at an average salary of $164,070. It also consists of things I take interest in. It requires a bachelors or some college. In this job the worker has to make plans, direct info or make activities coordinate in areas like electronic data processing, information systems, systems analysis, and computer programming. I would have to meet with department heads, managers, etc to resolve problems. Directing daily operations in this department, setting deadlines, analyzing workflows, settings priorities, etc is all required for this job.

Data Warehousing Specialists

in this job, you have to design, model, or implement corporate data warehouse activities. You have to program and tweak warehouses of database info and provide support to warehouse users. The personality required is one with persistence, cooperation, dependability, initiative, attention to detail, as well as analytical thinking. You usually will require a bachelor's degree when going into this field. Powerpoint is commonly used. Data modelers are also required for this field. Many verbal skills and problem solving skills are required as well. Mapping data, verifying structures, accuracy, and quality, of data.

Network & Computer Systems Administrators

You need to install, configure, and maintain an company's local area network, wide area network, data communications network, OSes, and physical and virtual servers. Execute system monitoring and check the integrity and inventory of hardware, network, and server resources and systems. You gotta develop data warehouse process models. This includes sources, loading, transorming, and extracting. The annual salary of this job $134,870. Powerpoint is also commonly used here. Metadata management software is used in this career.
